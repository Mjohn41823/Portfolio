Recipe Book

This is my recipe book. This allows the user to upload recipes to a digital recipe book. My goal was to create a go to place to people who have recipes written down on paper and to give a place to cooks who have family recipes he or she may have never digitized. This is written using Javascript, HTML and CSS.

2do list

This app is a daily to do list. This only focuses on today's list of things to get done. Most apps and websites allow the user to create lists for days in the future using a calendar system. This app is to focus on the to do list for the most important day of the week - today! It is written in Javascript, HTML and CSS.

Calorie Counter App

This application is the calorie counter for people who are trying to keep an eye on the daily calorie intake. This will allow the user to see how many calories they have taken in by logging the meals and snacks and gives an amount that is left in their daily alloted limit (set by the user). It is written in Javascript, HTML and CSS.
